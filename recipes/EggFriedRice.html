Egg Fried Rice

Description:

A delicious and easy-to-make egg fried rice with carrots and peas, perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably chilled)
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 1/2 cup peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped green onions for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble them until cooked. Remove the scrambled eggs and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the sesame oil and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced carrots and peas to the skillet, and stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the chilled cooked rice to the other side.
  6. Pour the soy sauce over the rice and toss everything together, allowing the rice to warm up and absorb the flavors.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Add the scrambled eggs back into the skillet and mix well with the rice and vegetables.
  9. Optional: Garnish with chopped green onions for added freshness and color.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
